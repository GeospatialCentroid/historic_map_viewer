**Key word explanations**

**General keywords**  
Not every map will need general key words. Keywords should only be added if 100% sure, such as the map being drawn in pencil.

* Origin  
  * handDrawn  
  * printed  
  * imagery  
* contourLines \- the map has contour lines but does not focus on elevation  
* plss \- Section, Township, Range lines

**Category keywords**  
Specific, easily identifiable features on the map. These key terms are broken into their respective categories to find them easier, but the category does not have to be included in the spreadsheet entry for these key terms to be added.

* Farming
